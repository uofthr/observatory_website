UTSC Observatory

The astronomical observatory at the University of Toronto Scarborough

Join the UTSC Observatory!

We have multiple telescopes ranging in size and quality. Recently, a new 8 inch telescope ws bought from the UTSC 50th anniversary legacy fund. The UTSC Observatory is currently maintained by Professor Hanno Rein. Whether you would like to get involved in operating the telescope or simply join us for one of the observing sessions, send him an e-mail to hanno.rein@utoronto.ca. All UTSC students, faculty and staff are welcome. Observing requires very clear skies which is hard to predict more than a day or two in advance. Therefore most observing sessions happen on short notice and will be announced via a mailing list.

Tumblr blog

We post observing logs and pictures on our tumblr blog at utsc-telescope.tumblr.com. The image on the right shows M51, the whirlpool galaxy. It was one of the first images taken here at UTSC with the 30cm telescope. Check out tumblr for more images.

Webcam

We have now a webcam installed in the telescope dome, looking down the valley. Watch the last one hour/24 hours as a loop.
